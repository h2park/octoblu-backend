Gateblu

Our Gateblu Gateway allows you to connect smart devices with IP addresses and not-so-smart devices without IP addresses to Meshblu via our software-based hub which supports Mac, Linux, & Windows

Mobiblu

    

Mobiblu is a cross-platform based mobile hub application enabling a powerful tool that controls most of the functions of Octoblu from your mobile device. Mobiblu allows the connection of plugins, any device or sensor to the app on the mobile device and then allows the triggering of a flow with the click of a button. Mobiblu also allows for the connection and control of Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices to the mobile hub.

Meshblu

Meshblu is a cloud-based MQTT & CoAP-powered network that scales to meet any needs whether the nodes are smart devices, sensors, cloud resources, drones, Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, etc

Blu

    

Behold the power of the Internet of things. Blu provides a dead simple way for Octoblu users to trigger a flows created in the Octoblu designer. With a simple click of a button, you can do just about anything.
