We are the creators of SKYNET.im - the popular open source machine-to-machine instant messaging platform!

SKYNET.im

SKYNET connects devices, systems, and people enabling real-time communications for the Internet of Everything via HTTP REST and WebSocket APIs. SKYNET operates as a global cloud-based platform but it can also be deployed as private cloud instances on home networks (including Raspberry Pis) and enterprise networks.

GitHub

SKYNET Hub

SKYNET now includes an open source, software-based home automation hub (similar to the Revolv and NinjaBlocks Sphere) that lets you connect devices with and without IP addresses to SKYNET! Our extensible plugin architecture allows you to connect *ANY* device to to SkyNet even older style serial or RF devices.

GitHub

SKYNET OS

SKYNET now offers a Device Operating System that lets you connect devices such as Arduino, Spark, and Pinoccio to SKYNET without a CPU! These devices simply auto-register and authenticate with SKYNET with a UUID & token and await instructions such as turning pins on/off or streaming sensor data to our cloud.
