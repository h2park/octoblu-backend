We are the creators of SKYNET.im - the popular open source machine-to-machine instant messaging platform!

  • SKYNET connects devices, systems, and people enabling real-time communications for the Internet of Everything via HTTP REST and WebSocket APIs. SKYNET operates as a global cloud-based platform but it can also be deployed as private cloud instances on home networks (including Raspberry Pis) and enterprise networks.

  • SKYNET now offers a home automation hub (similar to the Revolv and NinjaBlocks Sphere) that lets you connect devices without IP addresses to SKYNET!

  • SKYNET now offers an Arduino & Spark Operating System that lets you connect devices to SKYNET without a CPU!
